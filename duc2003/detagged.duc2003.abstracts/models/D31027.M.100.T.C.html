Iranian pro-democracy demonstrators protested the action of the conservative , 12-member Council of Guardians that rejected 214 of 400 candidates who wished to run in the national election for the 86-member Assembly of Experts . This assembly elects and supervises the country 's supreme leader , who has final say in all things . In vetting these aspirants , the council eliminated most reformist supporters of President Mohammad Khatami , a moderate clergyman with wide popular support . In the heavy boycotted election that followed , hard-liners , not surprisingly , won 54 of the 86 seats ; moderates won 13 , and independents 3. Sixteen seats went to candidates whose affiliation is unclear .
