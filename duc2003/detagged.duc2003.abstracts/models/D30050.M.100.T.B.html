Results of the 1998 U.S. Mid-Term Congressional elections will be far-reaching . Republicans have a long shot at gaining a filibuster-proof 60-seat majority in the Senate . Noted battles for Senator include D' Amato vs. Schumer in New York , and Moseley-Braun vs. Fitzgerald in Illinois . Republicans are often characterized as `` conservatives '' while Democrats are characterized as `` liberals . '' Political hybrids resist easy characterization as exemplified in D' Amato vs. Schumer . As Election Day nears , the House Judiciary Committee is considering impeaching President Clinton for alleged perjury and obstruction of justice in connection with the Lewinsky affair . The outcome of the elections is likely to influence deliberations about impeaching Clinton .
