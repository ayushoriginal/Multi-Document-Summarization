Typhoon Babs slammed into the Philippines in mid-October killing at least 150 people , injuring hundreds , and forcing hundreds of thousands to abandon homes damaged or destroyed by winds , floods , and landslides . Worst hit was a coconut-producing area whose damage toll subsequently caused a significant localized rise in cooking oil prices and contributed to inflationary pressures . Fund-raising campaigns began with a one-year salary donation by the Philippine 's president . The powerful storm also brought torrential rains and landslides to Taiwan where floods forced hundreds from their homes . Hong Kong was lashed with strong winds , forcing authorities to open shelters and close beaches .
