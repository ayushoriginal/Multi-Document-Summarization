The international war crimes tribunal in The Hague considered including Kosovo in its investigation of Yugoslavia . Milosevic denounced the tribunal , saying no crimes occurred . Investigators left without visiting Kosovo or interviewing a lone massacre survivor . Milosevic agreed to comply but reneged . The UN ruled investigators had authority throughout Yugoslavia , called a `` rogue state '' for refusing Kosovo access . Sanctions were threatened . Three judges and a new trial chamber were added and crimes on both sides investigated . Bosnian Muslim guards at Celebici prison camp were charged with murdering , torturing , raping and beating Serbian prisoners . A Bosnian Serb pleaded guilty to 14 murders , the tribunal 's third conviction . Pre-verdict sentencing arguments speeded tribunal work .
