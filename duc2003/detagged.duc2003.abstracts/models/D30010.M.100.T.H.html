Friday morning shoppers fled the Mahane Yehuda marketplace when a smoking car approached the entrance . It exploded , killing the bombers and injuring 24 , before explosives-filled suitcases could be carried into the crowd . Hamas claimed responsibility but Iranian-sponsored Islamic Jihad was later suspected . Israel 's Cabinet immediately suspended deliberations on the US-brokered Wye Accord , under which Israel would relinquish 13 percent more of the West Bank and Arafat would crack down on terrorists . Many doubted Netanyahu would let the accord be implemented , thus playing into militants ' hands . Arafat and Clinton urged that the attack not derail the accord . This third failure of an attempted attack suggested the crackdown on terrorists had produced results .
