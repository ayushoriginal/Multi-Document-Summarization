An Arctic cold wave brought unusually cold November weather across Europe . Southern Italy experienced snowfall and even Moscow , noted for cold weather , felt the wrath of the early cold spell . Many deaths were attributed to the cold with Poland apparently the hardest hit . The death toll there was greatest among the homeless , especially those who passed out drinking alcohol . In all , 85 deaths there were due to the cold where temperatures dipped as low as minus 26 degrees Celsius . Frostbite and hypothermia sent many to the hospital . Rail schedules throughout Europe were disrupted , ports were closed and many autos were stranded on snow-covered highways .
