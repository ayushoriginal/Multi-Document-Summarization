Election of members to serve in the Assembly of Experts occurred on 23 October 1998 . The 86-member cleric-based Assembly is important because it names and supervises the country 's supreme leader . The conservatives ' decisive victory was inevitable because the conservative-dominated 12-member Council of Guardians that passed judgment on the list of candidates rejected all except 30 of the moderate candidates . Moderate supporters of President Khatami publicly protested the action of the conservative screening council . Moderates threatened to boycott the election , and voter turnout was unusually low . Conservative hard-liners won at least 54 places on the 86-seat Assembly .
