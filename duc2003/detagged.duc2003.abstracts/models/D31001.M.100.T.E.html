The Truth and Reconciliation Commission , headed by Bishop Desmond Tutu , has issued its report on human rights violations in South Africa during apartheid . The report , which covers the years from 1960 to 1994 , lays a major portion of the blame on the ruling South African government during the period . All political parties , especially the African National Congress , also were accused of involvement in human rights violations . Former President de Klerk , who began the process to end apartheid in South Africa , was successful in having his name suppressed in the report . The African National Council also was considering bringing suit to have details of its role suppressed .
