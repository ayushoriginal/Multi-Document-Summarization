Fernando Cardoso was re-elected as Brazil 's President on 4 October 1998 . He received slightly more than the majority needed to insure a first round victory . Leftist da Silva received 34 % . There was some illegal late campaigning . Half of the country voted on shoe-sized computers that confused some older voters . Brazil is in dire economic straits , and Cardoso promised an austere program to ensure financial credibility . By slashing spending and raising taxes Cardoso hoped the country would qualify for an IMF loan . Many Cardoso allies were trailing in state level run-off races . Their defeats will hurt Cardoso . Pessimism pervaded the presidential inauguration .
