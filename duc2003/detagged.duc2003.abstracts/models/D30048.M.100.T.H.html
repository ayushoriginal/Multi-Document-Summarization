Losing $ 30 billion after Russia 's collapse triggered a currency and stock market collapse in Brazil , following failed economic projects , hyperinflation , large trade and budget deficits , and dependence on foreign capital . The IMF , the US , and Brazil finalized a $ 30 billion 3-year IMF package to stabilize Brazil 's economy , contingent on reforms . Clinton lobbied Congress for $ 18 billion , fearing Brazil 's troubles would affect the US . President Cardoso narrowly won re-election and pledged austerity measures to reduce the budget deficit . Brazil would raise $ 20 billion next year through spending cuts and taxes , 30 % from states and municipalities . The real will probably be devalued . Investors will watch for reforms and Brazil 's backing from wealthy nations .
