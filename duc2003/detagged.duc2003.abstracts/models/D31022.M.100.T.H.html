Sweden 's deadliest fire in decades , in an overcrowded discotheque with one available exit , killed 62 and injured 162 . Some jumped from windows . Most victims died from smoke inhalation . Almost all were immigrant teenagers representing 19 nationalities . The fire 's cause remains undetermined . Its speed and a report of teens ejected earlier suggest arson . Evidence also points to a short-circuit . Firemen , accused of slow response , arrived 6 minutes after deciphering a call in poor Swedish . Identifying victims was slow because many were too young for drivers licenses . A memorial was held at Goteborg Cathedral . The hall , rated for 150 but packed with 400 , was rented to Kurdish students who organized the party .
