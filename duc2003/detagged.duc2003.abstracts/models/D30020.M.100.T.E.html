Thailand is hosting the Asian Games and , with Tajikistan , Mongolia , and a Palestinian team expected to confirm , expects athletes from 39 countries , regions or organizations . Afghanistan , claiming financial problems , will not attend while North Korea , despite widespread hunger at home , will attend . Despite the loss of some veteran athletes and past drug problems , China hopes to do well in the medal standings even as it sends its first taekwondo team to the competition . Kazakhstan has hopes of taking the gold in women 's diving while India hopes to regain the top position it once held in world field hockey .
