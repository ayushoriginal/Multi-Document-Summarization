Thailand expects about 10,000 athletes to compete in its Asian Games in 1998 . China has 596 athletes at the games and hopes to win 100+ medals . It has also made sure its swimmers will avoid their drug scandals of previous Asian Games . The Chinese women divers expect to win gold over the highly competitive Kazakhstan competitors . North Korea , absent from the last games , has sent 307 athletes and officials . India hopes to defeat favorite South Korea in field hockey and regain its dominance in that sport . China , Turkmenistan , United Arab Emirates , and Thailand have won second-round soccer berths at the games .
