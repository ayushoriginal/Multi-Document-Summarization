A tapestry of associations link Osama bin Laden to the U.S. embassy bombings in Tanzania and Kenya and other terrorist acts . Salih Idris owned a Sudanese pharmaceutical plant with a suspected chemical weapons role and allegedly had ties with a bin Laden backed group . Fazul , accused of directing the Nairobi attack , reportedly led the Kenyan cell of bin Laden 's organization , Al Qaeda . While in Kenya , Fazul roomed with el Hage who was arrested in New York and charged with being a member of bin Laden 's network . The Taliban has refused U.S. and Saudi Arabian requests to deport bin Laden from Afghanistan .
