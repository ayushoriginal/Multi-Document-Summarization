Five days before money for the new fiscal year was to run out , Democrats accused the Republicans of deliberately moving slow on the 13 spending bills needed to keep the government from closing down . The situation was complicated by Republicans promoting bills with anti-environmental riders subject to veto by the President , plus demands for full funding for IMF . The Republicans began to back down , however , apparently to focus more on impeachment and to prepare for November elections . They compromised on environmental issues , a much smaller tax cut plan , and a conditional loan to the IMF to avert international economic crisis .
