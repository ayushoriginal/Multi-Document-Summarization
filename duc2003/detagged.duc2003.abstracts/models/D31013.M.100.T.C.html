The level of anti-abortion violence and terrorism seems to be increasing in the U.S. and Canada . Since 1993 , three doctors , three clinic employees and a clinic escort have been murdered and there have been more than a dozen attempted murders of abortion providers . Arson and bombings of clinics have become commonplace . The last victim of this violence is Dr. Barnett Slepian , an abortion provider at a public clinic in Buffalo , N.Y. Viewed by admirers as brave and conscientious , he was shot by a sniper . His death has become an anti-abortion violence issue in two N.Y. . State political office races .
