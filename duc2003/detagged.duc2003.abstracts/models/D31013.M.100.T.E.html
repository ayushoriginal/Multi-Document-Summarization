Dr. Barnett Slepian , a prominent Buffalo , New York obstetrician who provided abortions at a Buffalo clinic , was slain by a sniper 's bullet Friday night . The sniper , who fired through a kitchen window , shot Slepian as he talked with his wife and one of his sons . The attack on Dr. Slepian is the fifth by anti-abortion terrorists on doctors in the U.S. and Canada since 1994 . All of the attacks occurred around November 11th , Veterans Day in the U.S. and Remembrance Day in Canada , but marked as `` Remember the Unborn Children Day '' by anti-abortionists . The search of the sniper is continuing .
