At fiscal year 's end on Sept. 30 , 1998 , Congress had not approved 13 necessary spending bills , so voted emergency funding to keep the government running through Oct. 9th , then Oct. 12th . Members were eager to campaign at home for re-election but also to ensure funding for pet projects . Some bills risked veto because of anti-environmental riders . Several bills combined into an omnibus bill would make veto difficult . Republicans , vowing to avoid another shutdown that would distract attention from impeachment , became willing to resolve spending issues and dropped huge tax cut demands . Final unresolved issues included funding for the IMF and emergencies , and census technique . Clinton consulted other governments on proposed IMF changes .
