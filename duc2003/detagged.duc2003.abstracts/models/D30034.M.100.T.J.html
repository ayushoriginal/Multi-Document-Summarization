The East Timor Human Rights Center accused the Indonesian military of executing 50 East Timorese , possibly in retaliation for an attack on the troops by rebels in which three Indonesian soldiers died . The Indonesian Human Rights Commission said it would investigate the incident . Indonesian President Habibie proposed granting some autonomy to East Timor , which it invaded in 1975 and later annexed , in exchange for its recognition of Indonesian sovereignty . Independence activists have rejected the offer and call for a referendum . The U.N. never recognized Indonesian rule over East Timor and is mediating talks between Indonesia and Portugal , East Timor 's former ruler .
