As the end of the fiscal year draws near , congress has completed work on only four of the thirteen spending bills . Partisan bickering abounds as Republicans and Democrats find reasons to blame each other . Temporary extensions of current spending levels have been passed in order to keep the government running . Such delays are not uncommon since many congressmen hope to slip-in pet projects at the last minute to avoid critical notice . Republicans have compromised to concentrate on the coming impeachment hearings and believing that they can prevail in the next session . IMF finding and a tax cut proposal were measures where compromises are possible .
