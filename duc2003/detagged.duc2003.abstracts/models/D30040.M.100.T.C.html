Palestine , in a step toward statehood , opened and began operating its own airport in Gaza under accords brokered by President Bill Clinton . Israel retains control over Gaza airspace and security over arriving passengers and cargo . Jubilation erupted among Palestinian airport officials and passengers as the first Palestinian flight left Gaza~Rs Yasser Arafat International Airport bound for Amman , Jordan . The fledging Palestinian airline has two 50-seat Fokker 50s , and a Boeing 727 . They are flown by PLO pilots trained by other Arab countries and airlines . Israeli officials soon threatened to close the airport and delayed departure of some flights over security concerns .
