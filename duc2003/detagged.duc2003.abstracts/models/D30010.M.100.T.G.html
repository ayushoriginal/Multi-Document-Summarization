A car bomb exploded in a Jerusalem market Friday killing two assailants and wounding 21 Israelis . The militant group Hamas claimed responsibility . The incident caused the Israelis to suspend ratification of a U.S.-brokered Wye River accord calling for Israel to give Palestine 13 % of the West Bank in exchange for a Palestinian campaign against Islamic militants . Arafat promised to hunt the masterminds of the bombing and urged the Israelis not to halt the accord . Some believe the failure of the attack indicates Arab-Israeli efforts are weakening the militants . The president of Egypt is skeptical that the Israelis will implement the accord .
