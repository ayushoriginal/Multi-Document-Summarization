Pope John Paul II paid a three-day visit to Croatia in November 1998 . The primary purpose of this , his second , visit was the beatification ( the last step before sainthood ) of Cardinal Stepinac , who died in 1960 . The Pope declared Stepinac a martyr to the atrocities of Communism , but many non-Catholics considered him a nazi sympathizer during World War II. Almost 500,000 people attended the ceremony in Marija Bistrica . The Pope later met with President Tudjman in Zagreb and said a final mass in Split , where he called for reconciliation , a renewal of values , and increased international aid to the war-torn Balkans .
