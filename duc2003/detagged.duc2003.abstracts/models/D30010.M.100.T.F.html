A bomb-loaded car exploded near a busy Jerusalem market killing the bombers and injuring 21 others . Detonation was possibly premature , saving others from becoming victims . Hamas initially claimed responsibility but Islamic Jihad was the real agent . Israel immediately suspended action on the US-brokered peace accord . President Clinton expressed sympathy but said that the accord should not be derailed . Yassir Arafat appeared on Israel TV and condemned the attacks and promised to track-down the masterminds . Family members of the bombers expressed pride in their sons and confirmed that they were in Islamic Jihad . Egypt 's Mubarak expressed doubts regarding Israel 's peace efforts .
