Abdullah Ocalan 's Kurdish Workers Party fought for autonomy in Turkey since 1984 , causing 37,000 deaths . Turkey and the US labeled him a terrorist . Turkey threatened to attack Syria unless Ocalan was expelled . He fled to Russia and requested asylum . Russia 's parliament unanimously asked Yeltsin to grant it , while denying Ocalan 's presence . Turkey asked Russia to extradite him . Greek and Italian parliaments invited him to visit . Kurds in Armenia and Cyprus staged hunger strikes and demonstrated in his support . Ocalan flew from Moscow to Rome under a false passport . Italy had to arrest him on landing , given the serious charges . Ocalan requested Italian asylum . Italy has refused extraditions to capital punishment countries .
