Over 60 persons , mostly teens , were killed and scores injured in Sweden 's deadliest fire in modern times . The fire occurred in a Goteborg dance hall rented by a Macedonian immigrant group . Over 400 persons crowded the building approved for only 150 . Most at the Halloween hip-hop party were immigrants , or children of immigrants , from war ravaged or impoverished countries . Most victims choked on smoke and poisonous gasses . Survivors claimed response to the fire was slow but firemen were on the scene in six minutes . Cause of the fire has not been established but there were some hints of electrical problems and arson was also being considered .
