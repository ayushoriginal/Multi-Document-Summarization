The Justice department invoked the Sherman Antitrust Act , and brought civil suit against Microsoft , the world 's largest software producer . Twenty states also charged Microsoft . The trial began 23 October 1998 . The government charged that Microsoft sought Netscape Corporation 's collusion in restricting competition in the Internet software market . When Netscape refused , Microsoft attempted to thwart competition by bundling its browser with the highly popular Windows operating system . Microsoft denied all charges . The case is important . Microsoft 's loss could lead to increased watchdog scrutiny in the high-tech world . Microsoft 's attempt to acquire 3Com 's Palm Pilot indicated that their aggressive business practices would continue .
