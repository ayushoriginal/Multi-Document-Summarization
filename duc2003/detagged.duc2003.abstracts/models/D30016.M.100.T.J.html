Swissair announced that it was disconnecting the Interactive Flight Technologies entertainment system installed on its remaining MD-11s and Boeing 747s after investigators of the crash of Swissair Flight 111 found that the wires of the state-of-the- art system were heavily heat damaged . The flight , bound for Geneva from New York , crashed off the coast of Nova Scotia , killing all 229 people onboard . Swissair is among the carriers that has opted out of a $ 75,000 liability cap for people killed or injured during international flights and is looking into making payments of up to $ 137,000 to the families of the victims .
