With many Democrat seats in jeopardy , Republicans expect modest to high gains in both the House and Senate 1998 races . Several independent analysts also believe that Republicans will gain many of the tightly contested seats . In Illinois Republican Peter Fitzgerald holds a strong lead over the incumbent Democrat Senator Carol Mosely-Braun . Perhaps less typical is New York State , where Democratic challenger Schumer has battled to a statistical tie with incumbent Republican Senator D' Amato . As campaign tactics , both skewed their political ideology toward the center , with each accusing the other of extremism . The effect of President Clinton 's impeachment hearings on these elections is unknown .
