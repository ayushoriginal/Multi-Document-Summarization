Pope John Paul II , during a visit to Croatia , beatified the WW II cardinal of Zagreb , Alojzije Stepinac , for his staunch opposition to communism . Though a hero to Catholics , who comprise 80 percent of Croatia , Serbs view Stepinac as a wartime sympathizer with the pro-Nazi dictatorship that governed Croatia and killed thousands of Serbs , Jews , and Gypsies . Stepinac eventually denounced the atrocities , but to Serbs he is a symbol of Vatican cooperation with fascist leaders who are now being recast as Croatian patriots . Arrested in 1946 as a Nazi sympathizer , he was jailed and then released under house arrest in 1951 .
