Supertyphoon Babs , on the heels of Supertyphoon Zeb , brought heavy winds and rain to the Philippines , first hitting Catanduanes Island Thursday and stalling there 12 hours . Landslides and drownings killed at least 156 . 400,000 were driven from their homes . The two typhoons destroyed $ 100 million in crops , including abaca and coconut . President Estrada ordered riverside slums evacuated , facilitated emergency fund use and price freezing , and started a typhoon-victim fund . Heading toward southern China , Babs strengthened to sustained winds of 81 mph and gusts up to 100 mph . It brought torrential rains , flooding , and landslides to Taiwan and strong winds to Hong Kong before weakening to a severe tropical storm Sunday night .
