On Friday morning , November 6 , 1998 , a red Fiat blew up at a market in Jerusalem . The two bombers were killed and 21 others injured . Netanyahu immediately suspended discussions on ratifying the Wye Peace Accord , which would turn an additional 13 % of the West Bank over to Arafat . The Islamic Jihad was blamed for the blast , which exploded prematurely saving many lives . President Clinton , Secretary of State Albright , and Egyptian President Mabarak urged the Israelis to continue the ratification process . Arafat condemned the bombing and vowed to find the mastermind , but castigated Netanyahu for using the bombing as an excuse to derail the accord .
