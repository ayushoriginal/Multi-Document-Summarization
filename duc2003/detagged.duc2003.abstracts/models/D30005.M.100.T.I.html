Osama bin Laden , living in Afghanistan , was indicted in New York on November 3 , 1998 on 238 counts , including conspiracy to bomb the U.S. embassies in Tanzania and Kenya , committing acts of terrorism against Americans abroad , and leading a vast terror conspiracy . Two of the accused bombers were directly linked to bin Laden ; Al Owahali asked him for a `` mission '' in 1996 and el Hage was his aide in both the U.S. and Africa . A refuge center in Brooklyn was also directly linked to bin Laden and his violence . Islamic militants declared him a hero and threatened revenge . Saudi Arabia attempted to placate the U.S. and Afghanistan .
