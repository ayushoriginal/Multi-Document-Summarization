Turkish President Demirel will meet Deputy Premier Ecevit , fueling speculation that Ecevit will lead the government following Prime Minister Yilmazi 's ouster over corruption charges . Ecevit proposed a new secular coalition combining his party with those headed by Yilmaz and former premier Ciller . Reportedly , Ecevit is supported by Turkey 's powerful , pro-Western military . Traditionally , the Islamic Virtue Party would establish the new government since it holds the most seats in Parliament , but the constitution mandates a strictly secular republic . Furthermore , the military would not support an Islamic government . The government crisis comes during negotiations with Italy to extradite PKK rebel leader , Ocalan .
