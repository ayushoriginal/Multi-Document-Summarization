~ Indonesia invaded East Timor in December 1975 , a few months after the departure of Portugal 's colonial administration . It annexed the territory , 1,200 miles east of Jakarta , one month later . The United Nations still considers Portugal the administering power and is sponsoring peace talks between Indonesia and Portugal . Portugal and East Timorese pro-independence groups insist that a referendum be held to decide East Timor 's future . Pro-independence activists and Indonesian soldiers have clashed for years . In 1991 , Indonesian troops massacred at least 50 pro- independence protesters . Portugal is trying to extradite former President Suharto for alleged human rights abuses committed under his authoritarian rule .
