The Leonid meteor shower that occurred on 16 and 17 November 1998 consisted of small fragments from the Comet Temple-Tuttle . Some scientists predicted that the 1998 meteor shower would be intense . Asia was expected to be the best viewing area . The display in Asia was disappointing , but was unexpectedly good over southern Europe . Chances of severe damage to any private or government were considered minimal . Nevertheless , operators of the 600 plus satellites that relay national defense information , entertainment and news stories took cautionary steps to further reduce risk . There were no reports of damage to any satellite .
