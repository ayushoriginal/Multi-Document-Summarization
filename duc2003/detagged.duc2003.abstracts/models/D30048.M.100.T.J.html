Many issues and sometimes conflicting strategies for stabilizing the global economy surfaced at the October IMF and World Bank meeting . Japan announced a multi-billion dollar program for Asian nations . Russia threatened to default on loans unless it received the billions that were suspended by the fund . The U.S. sought to change the role of IMF from disaster relief to before crisis assistance . Brazilian officials negotiated for an IMF bailout of at least $ 30 billion . Though IMF managing director Camdessus supported the aid package , the Brazilian government , led by recently reelected President Cardoso , must take serious steps to reduce the budget deficit .
