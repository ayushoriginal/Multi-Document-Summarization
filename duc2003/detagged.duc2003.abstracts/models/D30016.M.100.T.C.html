Swissair flight 111 crashed off Newfoundland on September 2 , 1998 after leaving New York for Switzerland . The pilots reported smoke in the cockpit 16 minutes before the crash . All 229 people aboard died . Swissair is compensating and further assisting the grieving families . Eventually , over 90 percent of the plane , a 7-year-old MD-11 which last had major maintenance 13 months earlier , was recovered . Wreckage showed evidence of high heat and heat damaged wiring above the cockpit area but investigators remain unsure of its cause . The planes ' in-flight entertainment system is suspect . A final determination of the crashes cause is still far off .
