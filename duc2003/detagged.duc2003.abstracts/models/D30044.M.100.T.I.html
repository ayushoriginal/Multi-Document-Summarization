Abdullah Ocalan , the leader of the Kurdish Workers Party ( PKK ) , was detained on November 12 , 1998 , in Rome as he got off of a flight from Moscow . Turkey , where he is on trial in absentia , is seeking his extradition . Germany also has issued an arrest warrant . The PKK has been fighting Turkey for autonomy since 1984 . After heavy Turkish pressure , Syria had expelled Ocalan on October 21 . Although his location was not known for several weeks , he sought political asylum in Russia . He is now expected to ask for asylum in Italy . Ocalan 's odyssey has spawned demonstrations in Armenia and Cyprus .
