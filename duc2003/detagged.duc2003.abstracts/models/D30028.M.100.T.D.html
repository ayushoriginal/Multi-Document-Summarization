In October 1998 Turkish-Syrian dissension reached the level of undeclared was . Turkey massed troops on the Syrian border , and Turkish jets buzzed the border . Turkey has long accused Syria of aiding Syrian-based Kurdish rebels who make cross-border raids on Turkey . Syria denies this and accuses Turkey of illegally diverting water from flowing into Syria . Israel and Turkey signed military accords in 1995 , and Syria says that the two are conspiring against Syria . Israel denies this . Turkey and Syria have also quarreled over a territorial issue . Egyptian President Mubarak worked to ease tensions . He led discussions in Ankara and Damascus .
