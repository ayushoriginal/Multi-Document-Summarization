The Gaza International Airport opened on 24 November 1998 after a delay of two years . The airport was stipulated by the Clinton-brokered Wye River Accords . Jordan , Morocco , Spain and the Netherlands helped finance its construction . Palestinians were elated , considering the airport as a potential economic lifeline , and a step toward statehood . Israel still controls the air space and oversees security . Early operations were disrupted by Israeli-Palestinian security disputes . The first Palestinian commercial flight occurred on 5 December 1998 . The Palestinian Airlines is tiny , but regular flights to Jeddah , Cairo and Amman are planned , and eventually pilgrimage flight to Mecca .
