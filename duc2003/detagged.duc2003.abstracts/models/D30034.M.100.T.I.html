After several months of relative calm , killing resumed on October 30 , 1998 in the disputed East Timor territory . Three Indonesian soldiers were killed amid rumors that the military was increasing its presence in the territory . Indonesia , which annexed the territory in 1975 , had offered East Timor some autonomy , but would retain control of defense , foreign , and economic policy . The East Timorese were seeking a referendum . The United Nations was trying to mediate an agreement between Portugal and Indonesia . Commemorations of the November 11 , 1991 massacre added to the tensions . Both sides accused the other of mounting new offensives and killings , kidnappings , and demonstrations continued .
