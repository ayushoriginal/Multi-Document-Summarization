Syria acceded to Turkish demands that it stop supporting the rebellious PKK and expel the group 's leader , Abdullah Ocalan , averting a military crisis . Turkey calls the PKK , which seeks an autonomous Kurdish state in Turkey , a terrorist organization and Ocalan the country 's most wanted criminal . Nearly 37,000 people have died in the conflict . Ocalan , the subject of a Turkish international arrest warrant , was detained in Rome after arriving there from Moscow where he had sought asylum . It is uncertain whether Italy will agree to Turkey 's extradition request . Meanwhile , hunger strikes to protest Ocalan 's detainment have occurred in Nicosia and Armenia .
