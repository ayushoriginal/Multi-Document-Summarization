As many as 10,000 athletes were expected to participate in the Asian Games held in Bangkok , Thailand . Even impoverished North Korea planned to send 317 athletes and officials . Afghanistan was expected to be the only country certain not to attend . China despite missing some of its stars is expected to be the powerhouse team . Steroid use by Chinese swimmers in the previous Games is now thought to no longer a factor . India hopes to show well in field hockey , but South Korea is the favorite . China , Turkmenistan , UAE , and Thailand advanced in second round soccer . China , as expected , dominated aquatic events .
