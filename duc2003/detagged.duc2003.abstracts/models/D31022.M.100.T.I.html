The worst fire in Swedish history occurred on October 29 , 1998 , when a Goteborg dancehall , jammed with teenaged immigrants , burst into flame . By November 2 , the death toll stood at 62 , with 162 injured , 17 of whom were still in intensive care . Officials had not yet determined the cause . Four hundred people had been crowded into a hall approved for only 150 . Fire blocked one exit and the other exit contained furniture . Rescuers took 6 minutes to arrive , after having trouble understanding the accented call for help , but the fire spread very rapidly . The dead included individuals with 19 different nationalities .
