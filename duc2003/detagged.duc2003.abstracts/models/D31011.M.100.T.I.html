Typhoon Babs hit the Philippine Islands on October 21 , 1998 , with 150 mph winds causing flooding , landsides , and flying debris . Hundreds of thousands had to flee their homes , 156 were killed , and $ 100 million worth of crops were damaged . Parts of the northern and central areas were declared a `` state of calamity '' , and , on October 29 , a fund-raising campaign to aid the victims was begun . After leaving the Philippines , Babs moved over the South China Sea , hitting Taiwan on the 25th with heavy rains and winds and pushing strong winds into Hong Kong . Later in the day , Babs weakened to a severe tropical storm .
