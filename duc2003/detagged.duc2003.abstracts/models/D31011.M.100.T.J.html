Cooking oil prices rose sharply in the Philippines on speculation that copra production will decline due to the effects of el Nino and recent typhoons on coconut crops . Typhoon Babs , which caused 151 fatalities and forced 400,000 people from their homes , was particularly destructive in the Bicol region , a large coconut-production area . After Babs , President Estrada declared four provinces and a city to be calamity zones , eligible for emergency funds . He also jump-started a fund for victims of the typhoons with a contribution equivalent to his yearly salary . Babs subsequently swept through Taiwan , triggering landslides , and Hong Kong .
