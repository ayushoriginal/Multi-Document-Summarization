A U.S. federal court returned a 238-count indictment against Saudi exile Osama bin Laden for conspiring to bomb two U.S. embassies in Africa and committing other acts of terrorism . Evidence was compiled mainly from determination of links to bin Laden by suspects indicted in connection with the Embassy attacks as well as other previous terrorist activities . At the urging of the U.S. , Saudi Arabian officials attempted to persuade the Taliban 's leader to deport in Laden from Afghanistan , but he refused . Pakistani Muslim militants threaten retaliation if their `` hero '' is arrested . The U.S. is offering a $ 5 million reward for information leading to his arrest .
