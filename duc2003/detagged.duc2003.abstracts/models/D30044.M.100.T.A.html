In October 1998 , under pressure from Turkey , Syria expelled Abdullah Ocalan , leader of the Kurdistan Workers Party ( PKK ) , identified as a terrorist organization . . Ocalan was rumored to be in Russia . Turkey asked Russia to extradite Ocalan , but Russia would not even confirm his presence there . In mid-November Ocalan suddenly arrived in Rome on a flight from Moscow and was immediately arrested . Turkey called for Ocalan=s extradition from Italy . Although as a NATO ally Italy might grant Turkey 's request , it had consistently refused to extradite anyone facing the death penalty as Ocalan clearly would in Turkey , so the outcome remained in doubt .
