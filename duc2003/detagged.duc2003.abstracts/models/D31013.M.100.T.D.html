Dr. Barnett Slepian was murdered in his suburban Buffalo home on 23 October 1998 . He maintained a private obstetrics practice , and worked regularly at Buffalo 's only public abortion clinic . Snipers had attacked five abortion providers in New York and Canada over the past four years . Slepian had been warned that he was endangered . Abortion rights advocates decried Slepian 's murder , and blamed opponents , including some politicians , of creating a climate that led to the killing . Slepian 's murder was a factor in the New York Attorney General and senatorial campaigns . Investigators reported little progress . The FBI was aiding local police .
