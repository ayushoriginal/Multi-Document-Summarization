Brazilian President Cardoso won his first-round re-election because voters trusted his economic management more than that of his chief rival . However , Brazil has serious financial problems due to money depreciation and large trade and budget deficits , particularly after becoming caught up in the financial turmoil that began in Southeast Asia with foreign investment pullouts . The IMF and the U.S. could provide an aid package pending evidence of a promising economic recovery plan that would cut government expenses . Gubernatorial elections will be important because governors control a considerable share of the national budget and could be told to cut local budgets .
